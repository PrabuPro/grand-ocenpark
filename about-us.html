About Us

Grand Ocean Park Negombo, is the go to place, for anyone in the mood for a delightful meal. Our mouth-watering dishes, brought to you with just the right mix of fresh aromatic spices, fresh ingredients and expert culinary skills is surely a treat to savour. Serving a wide range of cuisine - be it Sri Lankan, Chinese, Indian, Asian, Arabian, Italian, and Mexican or continental - our dishes are sure to embark your senses in a most fascinating culinary journey.

The menu features a variety of signature fresh seafood dishes with crab, lobster, prawn and an array of fish. Highlighting, yet not limiting to seafood, we serve tantalising meat items and much sought after vegetarian options. The ambiance of Grand Oceanpark, is inviting and unique. Relax and unwind at the modern ‘chill the evening away’ set up overlooking the busy colourful sea street,

listening to the tunes of soothing music while the big flat screen plays interesting documentaries from culinary highlights from around the world.’ We are excited to make your meal special, not only in terms of great food, but though a wonderfully relaxing experience with your friends and family that will keep you coming back for more.
